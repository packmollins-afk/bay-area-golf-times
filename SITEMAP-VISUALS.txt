## 9. URL SLUG GENERATION

Course Name → Slug Transformation

"TPC Harding Park"
  → lowercase: "tpc harding park"
  → remove special chars: "tpc harding park"
  → replace spaces: "tpc-harding-park"
  → remove duplicates: "tpc-harding-park"
  FINAL URL: /course/tpc-harding-park

"Corica Park - South Course"
  → lowercase: "corica park - south course"
  → remove special chars: "corica park  south course"
  → replace spaces: "corica-park--south-course"
  → remove duplicates: "corica-park-south-course"
  FINAL URL: /course/corica-park-south-course
